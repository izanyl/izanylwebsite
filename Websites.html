Timeline of Pink Dolphin (Brand) Fanpage

This website consisted of creating a timeline of something or someone that we like a lot. The idea was to create a scrolling page filled with animations and effects. I ended up choosing a clothing brand that I have always been a fan of. The Brand's name is called 'Pink Dolphin' which consist of street wear, vintage, and vivid colors. My idea was to try and animate my fanpage website as best as I could since it was my oppurtunity to go above and beyond. Anyway, I learned many new skills when I was editing this site and even created my own gif as the background. Unfortunaly, some items did not go as planned because of sizing issues or time conflict. Other than that, I am proud of how the result came out to be in the end.
